Using an existing Corporate Account

If your organization already has access to a "Universal Credit" Cloud Account, you must identify the person in your organization who has the "Cloud Administration" account. This person can declare you as a new user on your organization's Cloud Instance, and grant you the required priviledges to execute these labs.

If you have problems to find your "Cloud Administrator" then please join our Q&A sessions.


Using a Trial Account

Oracle offers a free Cloud Trial, with 300$ free credits. Use the link below to navigate to the registration page, where you will need to enter some details about yourself, including a credit card. Please note no charges will be applied on this card, this is just a precaution to avoid abuse of the Trial environments. Your explicit consent will be requested in case you would like to convert your Trial account into a "Pay-as-you-go" account.
